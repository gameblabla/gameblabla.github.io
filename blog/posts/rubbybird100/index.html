Rubby Bird 100 - Official Release

Posted at — Sep 29, 2016

I am proud to announce the release of Rubby Bird 100, the sequel to Rubby Bird !

You thought rubbing one bird was easy enough ? Try with 100 birds instead !

The darker they become, the closer they are to death. If you fail to keep alive with a certain amount of birds, it’s game over !

Rubby Bird 100 is available for Android, Sailfish OS and browsers at Newgrounds and Kongregate.
